Our Mission

To create opportunities for African youth by training them on digital, software and technological skills. This
prepares them for the future of work by equipping them with marketable, industry driven skills.

Our goal is to ensure that our talented youth are available and trained in order to meet the growing
opportunities and needs of the ICT Industry in Africa.

We also envision an explosion of technology innovation and the creation of successful tech ventures by
local developers from across the country with a zeal for changing the world through harnessing the power
of their technology training for social good.
